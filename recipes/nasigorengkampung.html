Nasi Goreng Kampung

Description

Fastest way to cook Nasi Goreng Kampung

Ingredients

Ingredients for paste (A)

  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp fried anchovies
  • 10g (0.3 oz) of belacan
  • 6 birdâ€™s eye chili (red and green mix)
  • 4 shallots, about 40g

Other Ingredients (B)

  • Cooking oil
  • 60g (2 oz) Kangkong (water spinach)
  • 50g (1.75 oz) long beans
  • 10g (0.3 oz) ikan bilis (about one handful)
  • 100g (3 oz) chicken breast meat 60g, cut into dice
  • 2 egg
  • 300g (10 oz) cooked rice

Seasoning

  • 2 tbsp kicap manis (Indonesian sweet soy sauce)
  • 1/4 tsp ground white pepper
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Remove the head and gill of the anchovies. Rince. Drain. Dry on kitchen towel Fill the pan/wok with 1/4 of cooking oil. Deep-fried the dried anchovies in hot oil until crispy.
  2. Put on a kitchen towel to remove excess oil. Set aside or keep for future use.
  3. Blend ingredient A. Then saute in the remaining oil after deep-frying the anchovies.
  4. Add the chicken cubes. Stir-fried over medium heat until the chicken is cooked. Remove from the wok.
  5. Add some oil to stir-fry the cooled and dried cooked rice over low to medium heat for one minute. Push the rice to the side of the wok. Add two eggs. Scrambled the eggs until it is nearly cooked. Then push the rice back to the center and combine with the eggs.
  6. Return the chili paste and chicken to the wok.
  7. Add the long beans and kangkung.
  8. Season with C.
  9. Turn to high heat to stir fry for half a minute.
  10. Garnish and serve.
